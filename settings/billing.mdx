## Accessing Billing

Navigate to **Settings → Billing** from the sidebar.

<Note>
Only users with Admin role can view and manage billing settings.
</Note>

## Credit System

Email PO processing uses a credit system:

| Action | Credits Used |
|--------|--------------|
| Email received & parsed | 1 credit |
| Order created from email | Included |
| Failed parsing (retry) | No charge |

### Buying Additional Credits

If you run out of credits before your billing cycle resets:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Buy Credits**
3. Select a credit pack
4. Complete payment

Credit packs don't expire and roll over between billing cycles.

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade to a higher tier:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Plans">
    Click **Change Plan** to see options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    Choose the plan that fits your needs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Upgrade">
    Review prorated charges and confirm
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access New Features">
    New limits and features apply immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Prorated Billing

When you upgrade mid-cycle:
- You're charged the difference for remaining days
- New limits apply immediately
- Next full charge at regular billing date

## Downgrading Your Plan

<Warning>
Before downgrading, ensure your usage is within the new plan's limits.
</Warning>

To downgrade:

1. Click **Change Plan**
2. Select the lower tier
3. Review what you'll lose access to
4. Confirm the downgrade

Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. You keep current access until then.

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Click **Add Payment Method**
2. Enter card details or connect bank account
3. Verify the payment method
4. Set as default (optional)

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- ACH bank transfer (US only)

### Updating Payment Method

1. Find the payment method in the list
2. Click **Edit** to update details
3. Or click **Remove** to delete it

<Note>
You must have at least one valid payment method on file to maintain your subscription.
</Note>

## Tax Information

### Adding Tax ID

For business tax purposes:

1. Click **Edit Tax Info**
2. Enter your Tax ID / VAT number
3. Save changes

Your Tax ID will appear on future invoices.

### Tax Exemption

If your organization is tax-exempt:

1. Click **Upload Tax Exemption**
2. Upload your exemption certificate
3. Our team will review and apply it

## Canceling Your Subscription

To cancel your Buddy subscription:

1. Click **Cancel Subscription**
2. Select your reason for canceling
3. Confirm cancellation

When you cancel:
- Access continues until the end of your billing period
- Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation
- You can reactivate anytime during the retention period

<Tip>
Consider downgrading instead of canceling—you can always upgrade again later without losing your data.
</Tip>
