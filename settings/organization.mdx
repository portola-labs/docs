## Company Profile

### Basic Information

Configure your company details:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Company Name** | Your business name (shown on invoices) |
| **Legal Name** | Official registered name |
| **Address** | Primary business address |
| **Phone** | Main contact number |
| **Email** | Primary contact email |
| **Website** | Your company website |

### Business Details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Industry** | Your business sector |
| **Tax ID** | Business tax identification number |
| **Currency** | Default currency for pricing |
| **Timezone** | Your business timezone |

### Logo Requirements

- **Format**: PNG, JPG, or SVG
- **Size**: Minimum 200x200 pixels
- **File Size**: Maximum 2MB

Your logo appears on:
- Generated invoices
- Email communications
- Exported documents

## Preferences

### Order Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Default Order Status** | Initial status for new orders |
| **Order Number Prefix** | Custom prefix (e.g., "SO-") |
| **Require PO Number** | Whether orders need a PO reference |
| **Auto-generate Invoice** | Create invoice when order completes |

### Product Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Default Unit** | Default unit of measure |
| **Show SKU** | Display SKU in product lists |
| **Price Decimal Places** | Precision for pricing (2-4) |

### Customer Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Default Pricing Tier** | Tier for new customers |
| **Require Phone** | Whether phone is required |
| **Require Address** | Whether address is required |

## Pricing Tiers

Manage your customer pricing tiers:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Tier">
    Click **Add Tier** to create a new pricing level
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Discount">
    Set the default discount percentage for this tier
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Priority">
    Arrange tiers in order of discount (lowest to highest)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Common Tier Examples

| Tier | Typical Discount |
|------|------------------|
| Retail | 0% (base price) |
| Wholesale | 20-30% |
| Distributor | 30-40% |
| VIP | 40-50% |

<Tip>
You can override tier pricing for individual products or customers from their respective pages.
</Tip>

## Data & Privacy

### Data Export

Export all your organization data:

1. Click **Export All Data**
2. Select what to include (customers, products, orders)
3. Choose format (CSV or JSON)
4. Download the archive

### Data Retention

Configure how long to keep certain data:

| Data Type | Default Retention |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Activity Logs | Indefinite |
| Deleted Records | 30 days |
| Import History | 90 days |

## Danger Zone

<Warning>
These actions are irreversible. Proceed with caution.
</Warning>

### Delete Organization

Permanently delete your organization and all data:

1. Click **Delete Organization**
2. Type your organization name to confirm
3. Enter your password
4. Click **Permanently Delete**

This action:
- Deletes all customers, products, orders, and invoices
- Disconnects all integrations
- Removes all team members
- Cannot be undone
