## Get Started in Minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up at [app.usebuddy.io](https://app.usebuddy.io) and verify your email
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your tools" icon="link">
    Link Shopify, QuickBooks, or import your data from spreadsheets
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start processing orders" icon="rocket">
    Create orders manually or forward PO emails for automatic processing
